title: "About Waldi Lane Farm"
---

Our family farm is located in a pretty valley outside the quaint town of Winlock, Washington. We primarily raise mini nubian dairy goats. On our homestead, we keep a variety of laying hens and occasionally meat birds. To protect them all, we have several livestock guardian dogs. The barn cats manage the whole operation. We grow fruits and vegetables and as many roses as I can sneak in. Lastly, no barn is complete without the money pit that is my beloved horses.
