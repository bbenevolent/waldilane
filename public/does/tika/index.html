Tika

Registered: Floof Farm Tequila Sunrise

📷 Photo coming soon

Tika is a gorgeous Nigerian Dwarf. She is level and wide. She has a perfect brisket and proper rear leg angulation. Her best characteristic is her personality. She knows she is adorable. She is another one of my 2025 kids that loves to follow me around. She comes from a wonderful line of high performers. Her dam, Savy is my favorite showing partner and I look forward to showing Tika in 2026. Thank you to Kate at Floof Farm for selling this gorgeous girl to me.
