Welcome to Our Farm

Waldi Lane is located in the quaint town of Winlock, WA, home of the World's Largest Egg. At Waldi Lane we run a performance herd of dairy goats. We have TMGR and MDGA registered Mini Nubians and ADGA Nigerian Dwarfs and Nubians. Our dairy goats are selected for production, conformation, and charming personality.

SW Washington Strong

Happy · Healthy · Productive

🐐 Happy

Our kids are handled on a daily basis. They love the human kids and everyone else who might give them a bottle or a hug (or both!)

🥛 Healthy

We participate in DHI and have our does on test every year. We breed for production and correctness in udders.

✅ Productive

We test our herd for common caprine diseases annually and we maintain an active working relationship with our local livestock vets to maintain tip top health.

Why You'll Love Us

Friendly · Knowledgeable · Helpful

There has never been a better time to start raising dairy goats. These amazing animals are fun and practical. Our mini nubians have sweet tasting creamy milk that is excellent for drinking plain, making sauces, ice cream, or soap. These guys will manage your brush, trim your trees, and mow your lawn. We have goats of all ages available. Our family has just the right goats for you!
