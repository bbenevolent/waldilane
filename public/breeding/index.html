2026 Breeding Plan

Below are the planned breedings for the 2026 kidding season. You will notice that I am breeding back to foundation (first generation) on a number of these pairings. Not only am I breeding back to foundation, but I am breeding to Nigerian Dwarf bucks rather than using a Nubian doe bred to a mini nubian buck. I am very conscientious of keeping size down. One of the goals of Mini Nubian breeding is to have goats that are half the size of a full Nubian while retaining the Nubian breed characteristics and higher capacity. I have confidence that the ladies I have chosen will carry forward the most desirable qualities and are well paired with the excellent bucks I have been fortunate to use courtesy of Floof Farm.
